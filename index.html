About

J.R. Hernandez

I built this practice for people who want real answers and a clear way forward. My work is process-based, case-specific, and consultation-led. We define the problem precisely, reduce noise, and focus on what is most likely to change the outcome.

You will always know what we are focusing on, what the next step is, and what progress should look like in your day-to-day life. Confidentiality and professional boundaries are central, and I am explicit about what this work can and cannot do — so you can make informed decisions with confidence.

I have spent the last decade living across six countries on three continents — Venezuela, Chile, France, the United States, Singapore, and Russia. I understand the specific pressures of building a life far from home: the reinvention, the invisible exhaustion, the grief you carry while performing competence. That experience is a clinical advantage.

Approach

How This Work Is Structured

Clear assessment, practical next steps, and follow-through — built around what actually fits your case.

01

Consultation-Style Assessment

We map symptoms, habits, history, and physiology to identify what is maintaining the problem — so the intervention targets the right mechanisms from the start.

02

Focused Intervention Plan

Based on what the assessment reveals, we build a targeted plan drawing from CBT, ACT, and somatic regulation — adapted to your specific case, with clear priorities and direction.

03

Track and Adjust

Progress is measured in observable terms: fewer spirals, better sleep, faster recovery after triggers, more reliable follow-through. If something is not working, we adjust. Every week.

Specializations

What I Help With

If what you are dealing with is on this list, you are in the right place. If it is not, we will map what is happening and decide the next step.

Burnout & Chronic Stress

Your system has been running on overdrive for so long that rest alone does not fix it. Exhaustion, irritability, fog, and a nervous system stuck on alert.

Anxiety & Nervous System Dysregulation

Racing thoughts, tension, hypervigilance — your body stays in alert mode even when there is no immediate threat. The signal has gotten stuck.

Depression & Low Drive

Flatness, withdrawal, loss of motivation. A system that has shut down to protect itself, now making daily functioning harder than it should be.

Emotional Regulation

Reactions that feel disproportionate, emotional numbness, overwhelm — emotions that run you instead of informing you. Regulation can be trained.

Relationship Patterns

Repeating cycles of conflict, emotional distance, avoidance, or the same argument with different words. The pattern is the problem, and patterns can be mapped.

Life Transitions & Identity

Relocation, breakups, career shifts, grief, cultural adjustment — rebuilding stability and direction when the ground shifts beneath you.
